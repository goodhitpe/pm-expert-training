## Official PM Organizations

### Global Organizations

#### Project Management Institute (PMI)
- **Website:** https://www.pmi.org
- **Description:** World's leading professional association for project management. Offers certifications (PMP, CAPM, PMI-ACP), standards (PMBOK), and resources.
- **Key Resources:**
  - PMI Standards+ (digital library)
  - PM Network Magazine
  - ProjectManagement.com
  - Local chapter events
- **Cost:** $139/year membership

#### Scrum Alliance
- **Website:** https://www.scrumalliance.org
- **Description:** Global organization dedicated to Scrum and agile practices. Offers CSM, CSPO, and other Scrum certifications.
- **Key Resources:**
  - Articles and case studies
  - Webinars and events
  - Trainer directory
  - Community forums
- **Cost:** Free to browse; membership included with certification

#### Scrum.org
- **Website:** https://www.scrum.org
- **Description:** Founded by Scrum co-creator Ken Schwaber. Offers PSM, PSPO, and other Professional Scrum certifications.
- **Key Resources:**
  - Scrum Guide (free, official)
  - Open Assessments (free practice tests)
  - Forums and discussions
  - Learning paths
- **Cost:** Free resources; pay per certification exam

#### Axelos (PRINCE2)
- **Website:** https://www.axelos.com
- **Description:** Owns PRINCE2, ITIL, and other best practice frameworks. Popular in UK and Europe.
- **Key Resources:**
  - PRINCE2 methodology guides
  - Accredited training providers
  - Best practice whitepapers
- **Cost:** Varies by certification and training

#### International Project Management Association (IPMA)
- **Website:** https://www.ipma.world
- **Description:** Federation of national PM associations. Strong in Europe, Middle East, and Africa.
- **Key Resources:**
  - Individual Competence Baseline (ICB)
  - National association network
  - Research and publications
- **Cost:** Varies by country

#### Association for Project Management (APM)
- **Website:** https://www.apm.org.uk
- **Description:** UK's chartered body for project management professionals.
- **Key Resources:**
  - APM Body of Knowledge
  - Project journal
  - Webinars and events
- **Cost:** Membership required

#### Agile Alliance
- **Website:** https://www.agilealliance.org
- **Description:** Nonprofit organization supporting agile software development.
- **Key Resources:**
  - Agile glossary and guides
  - Experience reports
  - Agile conferences
  - Research papers
- **Cost:** Free resources; membership optional

## PM Tools and Software

### Comprehensive Project Management Tools

#### Jira (Atlassian)
- **Website:** https://www.atlassian.com/software/jira
- **Description:** Leading agile project management tool. Highly customizable for Scrum, Kanban, and hybrid approaches.
- **Best For:** Software development, agile teams, tech companies
- **Pricing:** Free (up to 10 users); Standard $7.75/user/month
- **Features:** Sprint planning, backlog management, reporting, integration with Confluence

#### Asana
- **Website:** https://asana.com
- **Description:** User-friendly work management platform. Great for collaboration and task management.
- **Best For:** Marketing teams, operations, general project management
- **Pricing:** Free (Basic); Premium $10.99/user/month
- **Features:** Task management, timelines, portfolios, automation

#### Monday.com
- **Website:** https://monday.com
- **Description:** Visual, customizable work operating system. Highly flexible with colorful interface.
- **Best For:** Creative teams, marketing, versatile use cases
- **Pricing:** From $8/user/month (3-seat minimum)
- **Features:** Custom workflows, automations, integrations, dashboards

#### Microsoft Project
- **Website:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/project
- **Description:** Traditional project management software with Gantt charts and resource management.
- **Best For:** Large enterprises, traditional waterfall projects, Microsoft ecosystem
- **Pricing:** From $10/user/month (Project Plan 1)
- **Features:** Gantt charts, resource leveling, critical path analysis

#### Trello
- **Website:** https://trello.com
- **Description:** Simple, visual Kanban-style board tool. Easy to learn and use.
- **Best For:** Small teams, personal projects, simple workflows
- **Pricing:** Free; Standard $5/user/month
- **Features:** Kanban boards, cards, checklists, power-ups (integrations)

#### ClickUp
- **Website:** https://clickup.com
- **Description:** All-in-one productivity platform. Combines docs, tasks, goals, and more.
- **Best For:** Teams seeking comprehensive, affordable solution
- **Pricing:** Free (generous); Unlimited $7/user/month
- **Features:** Tasks, docs, goals, time tracking, customizable views

#### Basecamp
- **Website:** https://basecamp.com
- **Description:** Simple project management and team collaboration. Flat-rate pricing.
- **Best For:** Small businesses, remote teams, simplicity seekers
- **Pricing:** $99/month flat rate (unlimited users)
- **Features:** To-dos, message boards, schedules, docs, file storage

#### Wrike
- **Website:** https://www.wrike.com
- **Description:** Versatile work management platform with powerful features.
- **Best For:** Marketing teams, professional services, enterprises
- **Pricing:** Free (limited); Professional $9.80/user/month
- **Features:** Gantt charts, custom workflows, proofing, analytics

#### Smartsheet
- **Website:** https://www.smartsheet.com
- **Description:** Spreadsheet-like interface with project management capabilities.
- **Best For:** Those who love Excel, construction, IT
- **Pricing:** Pro $7/user/month; Business $25/user/month
- **Features:** Grid, Gantt, card, calendar views; automation; dashboards
